<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">What are Reports</h3>

<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em;"><strong>A Report</strong> is a financial statement compiled by the system about the work done over a certain period of time.</p>

<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">What Types of Reports Exist</h3>

<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em;"><strong>1. Daily Report</strong> — it is compiled by the system from 00:00 MSK to 23:59 MSK. This report counts exclusively a 24-hour period, regardless of when you started the shift and when you finished it.</p>

<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em;"><strong>2. Partner Report</strong> — it is compiled by the system from the beginning of the work shift to its completion.</p>
