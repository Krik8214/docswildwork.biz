<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">FIN.CHAT</h3>

<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em;">This chat is intended for communication with the financial administrator: for topping up the insurance and working deposit, confirming financial reconciliations, withdrawing earnings, working and insurance deposits.</p>

<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em;"><strong>Chat Rules:</strong></p>
<ol style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 25px;">
  <li>Everyone in this chat communicates with respect — using "You" and without swearing.</li>
  <li>This chat is joined by invitation only.</li>
  <li>Chat working hours: during the daytime from 7:00 to 20:00, we top up and make withdrawals through the financial administrator. From 20:00 to 7:00, top-ups are made through support staff in the chat.</li>
  <li>Withdrawal is made from any amount.</li>
  <li>Withdrawal is made only on weekdays from 7:00 to 20:00 MSK. Saturday and Sunday — withdrawal is not made.</li>
  <li>Support staff do not make withdrawals — this is prohibited.</li>
</ol>

<ol style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 25px;">

  <li>This chat is joined by invitation only.</li>
  <li>This chat has an active conversation by support department employees. Ignoring a request may be regarded as team inactivity — support has the right to disable your in/out traffic.</li>
</ol>

<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">Regulations for Processing Incoming Deposit Requests (for Support and Operator)</h3>

<ol style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 25px;">
  <li>The processing period for deposit credits is 1 month from the date of formation. If the request has gone to the archive and the operator cannot provide a receipt or statement after the deadline, the merchant may be refused processing. Exceptions are requests previously directed by the merchant that were not processed.</li>
  <li>If the operator mistakenly confirmed a top-up request and the funds were not credited — contact support, specify the request ID and the essence of the problem. Support coordinates the cancellation of the request with the merchant.</li>
  <li>If the operator mistakenly confirmed a request for an amount different from the credited amount — contact support, specify the request ID. Support coordinates the amount correction with the merchant. The request will be declined only by agreement, cancellation is not guaranteed.</li>
  <li>If the client paid to details that do not belong to us — the deposit credit will be refused.</li>
<li>If client "1" paid an amount different from the request and this receipt automatically closed client "2's" request — the operator checks for a receipt from client "2". If there is a receipt — credit it to client "1's" request. If there was no payment from client "2" — the deposit credit to client "1" will be refused. This point is executed upon contacting and coordinating with support.</li>
  <li>If the client made a payment via SBP to a "different bank" — the payment must also be checked and credited to the deposit. In its absence — provide a statement. The team is responsible for the cleanliness of the details.</li>
  <li>To resolve disputed situations, support may request a statement from the operator for the period. Provision period — 2-5 days from the request (up to 7 days for a valid reason). Without a statement within the deadline, the request may be confirmed in favor of the client.</li>
  <li>The operator must provide a statement at support's request. Other documents (SMS, screenshots, video, audio) are not accepted. If the operator cannot provide a statement — the request must be confirmed. Otherwise, support may confirm it in favor of the client.</li>
  <li>Processing time for a request/dispute — 15 minutes, but no more than 30 minutes. If the team does not respond or ignores requests for more than 2 hours — traffic is removed until the circumstances are clarified.</li>
</ol>

<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">Regulations for ECOM AZ and E-wallet AZ</h3>

<ol style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 25px;">
  <li>The processing period for credits is 14 days. If the dispute came later, processing may be refused (except for previously directed requests).</li>
  <li>If the operator mistakenly confirmed a request and the funds were not credited — contact support with the request ID. Support coordinates cancellation, but it is not guaranteed.</li>
  <li>If the operator mistakenly debited an amount different from the request — contact support. Support coordinates the amount correction or partial/full refund with the merchant. This point also applies when support contacts the operator.</li>
  <li>If the client's funds did not reach the account — the deposit credit will be refused.</li>
  <li>If the account details are blocked — dispute processing up to 50 manat is done by the operator via receipt. Over 50 manat, the operator may request a statement from support. The operator does not provide a statement for these directions.</li>
  <li>Only detailed receipts are accepted. A screenshot of the transfer is grounds for refusal of processing.</li>
  <li>When processing a dispute via chat and there is no payment — provide a screenshot of the unsuccessful transaction or receipts for the period and a detailed answer.</li>
  <li>When processing via Telegram bot and there is no payment — upload a screenshot, provide a detailed answer, decline the dispute. If processed incorrectly, support may return the dispute for re-processing.</li>
</ol>

<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; margin-top: 15px;"><strong>Examples of detailed answers:</strong> "No receipt, operation error"; "No receipt in the transaction history"; "No receipt, the client did not send the code"; "No receipt, the client has no balance", etc.</p>
<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em;"><strong>Examples of incorrect answers:</strong> "No"; "Nothing like that"; "Can't find"; punctuation marks, etc.</p>

<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">Control of Team Responses — Trigger Factors for Suspending Work</h3>

<ol style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 25px;">
  <li><strong>Refusal to process.</strong> "No receipt" without evidence. Acceptable response: statement for the corresponding period + video from the bank history showing the card and payment times + screenshot from the Ecom Auris Bot. If the team provides evidence — provide it to the merchant. If evidence cannot be provided — the dispute is closed in favor of the client within 24 hours.</li>
<li><strong>Pattern of refusals from the team</strong> (no receipts). Recording refusals in the "Team Analytics" table for all teams and all products. Upon accumulating 5 refusals — notification to the "Support Review" chat for detailed analytics and re-checking.</li>
  <li><strong>Team ignoring</strong> — no response to a check for more than an hour. Traffic removal until communication is restored and checks are reviewed.</li>
  <li><strong>Intensive receipt of disputes</strong> (anomalous number of checks) — traffic removal until recovery and error correction.</li>
</ol>

<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">Unique Cases</h3>

<ol style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 25px;">
  <li><strong>Debiting of funds on a different date.</strong> Request confirmation from the team: a statement for the corresponding period for the corresponding card. Request from the merchant a video confirmation of the debiting of funds on the new date from the transaction history + a receipt with the new date.</li>
</ol>

<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">Regulations for Responding to Disputed Cases on Deposits and Payouts</h3>

<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em;"><strong>When responding to a deposit appeal, you must:</strong></p>
<ol style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 25px;">
  <li>For each support request, always specify the request ID and form a reply message to our request — for linking and saving the history in the Telegram chat.</li>
  <li>If the payment has been received and you have moved the request to successful status — provide a brief response: "Approved" or "Success" and specify the success amount.</li>
  <li>If there is no payment — provide a clear, coherent, and detailed answer with the reason for the absence of payment. <strong>Mandatory</strong> attach evidence: an informative video file from the recipient's personal bank account, showing the recipient's details, date, and time of payments. If the date and time are missing — you can drill into several payments for recording. If the debit was not made on the current day — attach a bank statement.</li>
  <li>The processing time for one appeal is <strong>no more than 15 minutes</strong>. The time can be extended to 30 minutes for an objective reason. If no response is provided within 45 minutes — the appeal may be closed in favor of the client. If there is no access to the requisite (if the requisite is blocked) — inform the support chat immediately, and support will request a statement from the client if possible.</li>
</ol>

<p style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em;"><strong>When responding to a payout appeal, you must:</strong></p>
<ol style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 25px;">
  <li>Check the client's statement.</li>
  <li>Check the payment refund. If the payment has been returned — notify support to cancel the request.</li>
  <li>If the payment was successfully made — provide a bank statement. Support may additionally request an informative video file from the sender's personal bank account.</li>
  <li>Processing time — <strong>no more than 15 minutes</strong>, up to 30 minutes for an objective reason. If there is no response within 45 minutes — the appeal may be closed in favor of the client.</li>
  <li>If it is impossible to confirm the payout with a successful receipt or statement — the case will be closed unilaterally in favor of the client.</li>
</ol>
