<h1 style="color: black; font-size: 2.2em; font-weight: bold; margin-bottom: 30px;"><span style="background-color: #d4edda; padding: 6px 20px; border-radius: 8px; display: inline-block;">❗️Important Rules and Notes</span></h1>

<p style="font-style: italic; color: black; font-size: 1.2em; margin-top: 0;">Great! We have added the requisite and made it active. Now let me tell you the main rules and notes on adding and activating a requisite.</p>

<h3 style="color: black; font-size: 1.5em; margin-top: 30px;">📋 Rules</h3>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>1. Rule:</strong></span> When adding a requisite, always select the correct gateway.</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>2. Rule:</strong></span> When adding a requisite, always select a partner.</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>3. Rule:</strong></span> When adding a card number, account, IBAN:</p>
  <ul style="color: black; font-size: 1.15em; padding-left: 30px; margin: 5px 0 0 0;">
    <li><strong>A)</strong> Copy and paste the requisite — we advise you not to write it manually!</li>
    <li><strong>B)</strong> Insert the requisite without spaces.</li>
    <li><strong>C)</strong> Always check the requisite you added to avoid errors.</li>
    <li><strong>D)</strong> If your requisite is not used for account or IBAN transactions — skip these points.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>4. Rule:</strong></span> Write the cardholder's name with a capital letter. Example: <strong>Kamil Mammedov</strong>.</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>5. Rule:</strong></span> Fill in the phone number with the "+" sign, country code, and the number itself. Example: <strong>+994567889333</strong>.</p>
  <div style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 8px 12px; margin-top: 8px; border-radius: 4px; border-left: 3px solid #007bff;">
    <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.05em; margin: 0;"><strong>Note:</strong> The phone number will be used for the automatic confirmation app. The ID for the app is your phone number. In the app, enter the phone number without the "+" sign.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>6. Rule:</strong></span> Always select your bank. If it's not there — contact TECH.CHAT and ask the administrator to add the bank you need.</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>7. Rule:</strong></span> If you added a requisite for the ECOM method, you do not need to specify the Telegram ID in the requisite.</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>8. Rule:</strong></span> Activate the requisite and immediately put it on pause.</p>
  <div style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 8px 12px; margin-top: 8px; border-radius: 4px; border-left: 3px solid #007bff;">
    <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.05em; margin: 0;"><strong>Note:</strong> After adding the requisite, you cannot immediately put it into operation.Before doing so, you need to check the app's functionality.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>9. Rule:</strong></span> After adding the requisite, install the automation app (step-by-step guide will be after fully studying the "Payment Infrastructure" section).</p>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>10. Rule:</strong></span> When the app is set up, make a test transfer to the requisite you added to work.</p>
  <div style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 8px 12px; margin-top: 8px; border-radius: 4px; border-left: 3px solid #007bff;">
    <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.05em; margin: 0 0 10px 0;"><strong>Note:</strong> After the test, the app should see the SMS or push notification, and it should appear in the app.</p>
    <ul style="color: black; font-size: 1.05em; padding-left: 20px; margin: 0;">
      <li><strong>PARSED</strong> — the requisite is working, can be activated.</li>
      <li><strong>SKIPED</strong> — SMS not added to the reader, contact TECH.CHAT.</li>
      <li><strong>OP_NOT_FOUND</strong> — ID does not match the requisite, the phone number was entered incorrectly.</li>
      <li><strong>REQ_NOT_FOUND</strong> — the requisite is filled in incorrectly.</li>
      <li><strong>READ TIMED</strong> — check your internet connection.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>

<div style="margin-bottom: 12px;">
  <p style="color: black; font-size: 1.25em; margin: 0;"><span style="background-color: #d1ecf1; padding: 3px 10px; border-radius: 4px;"><strong>11. Rule:</strong></span> When you have made a test transfer and it is successful — you can activate. When activated, the requisite should move to the "Active" tab.</p>
</div>
